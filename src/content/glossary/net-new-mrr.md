## Definition
Net new MRR is new MRR plus expansion MRR minus churned and contraction MRR.
## Why it matters
It is the clearest monthly growth signal for subscription businesses.
## Pricing implications
Price increases can raise net new MRR if churn stays below break-even. Monitor components separately.
## Measurement tips
Track net new MRR by segment to identify growth drivers.
## Checklist
- Separate new, expansion, churned, and contraction MRR.
- Compare net new MRR to starting MRR for growth rate.
- Track net new MRR by plan.
- Monitor net new MRR after pricing changes.
- Avoid mixing one-time revenue into MRR.
- Use consistent time windows.
- Review net new MRR monthly.
- Validate data with finance.
