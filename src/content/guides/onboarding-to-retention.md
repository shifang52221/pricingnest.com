## Quick checklist
- Map onboarding steps to month-1 retention.
- Track time-to-value by segment.
- Remove friction in the activation path.
- Tie onboarding success to expansion targets.
- Review onboarding weekly after pricing changes.

## Step-by-step
1. Define the activation event and time-to-value.
2. Map onboarding steps to activation.
3. Compare retention curves before and after changes.
4. Quantify churn impact by cohort.
5. Update onboarding content and tooling.

## What to watch
- Slow activation drives early churn.
- Onboarding gaps amplify price sensitivity.
- High-touch tiers need clear success plans.

## Common mistakes
- Ignoring cohort differences.
- Mixing trial and paid onboarding data.
- Optimizing for signup instead of activation.
