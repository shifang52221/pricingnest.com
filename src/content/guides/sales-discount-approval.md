## Quick checklist
- Define discount tiers by deal size.
- Require approval above set thresholds.
- Track discount impact on margin.
- Avoid stacking discounts.
- Review discount policy quarterly.

## Step-by-step
1. Set a standard discount ladder.
2. Define approval thresholds by tier.
3. Calculate effective monthly rates.
4. Track churn and renewal outcomes.
5. Adjust guardrails after pricing changes.

## What to watch
- Over-discounting erodes LTV quickly.
- Inconsistent approvals create leakage.
- Discounts without value messaging raise churn.

## Common mistakes
- Allowing ad-hoc discounts in enterprise deals.
- Ignoring gross margin impact.
- Mixing promotions with negotiated discounts.
