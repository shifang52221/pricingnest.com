## Quick checklist
- Show overage rates on the pricing page.
- Provide an example bill with overages.
- Alert customers before overages apply.
- Offer clear upgrade paths.
- Review overage disputes monthly.

## Step-by-step
1. Define overage units and rates.
2. Add examples in pricing docs.
3. Trigger alerts before caps.
4. Add in-app upgrade prompts.
5. Monitor churn after overage events.

## What to watch
- Hidden overages trigger chargebacks.
- Confusing units cause disputes.
- Overages without caps increase churn.

## Common mistakes
- Using different units across pages.
- Relying on terms only for disclosure.
- Ignoring seasonal usage spikes.
