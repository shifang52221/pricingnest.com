## Quick checklist
- Define caps in plain language.
- Explain what happens at the cap.
- Show examples that include caps.
- Align cap messaging across product and billing.
- Review cap policy after pricing updates.

## Step-by-step
1. Write a one-sentence cap definition for each plan.
2. Specify whether caps are hard or soft.
3. Add an example invoice with a cap.
4. Trigger in-product alerts before caps.
5. Train support on the cap policy.

## What to watch
- Caps that block core workflows.
- Inconsistent cap language between teams.
- Caps without an upgrade path.

## Common mistakes
- Hiding cap details in legal documents.
- Using caps that are lower than included usage.
- Failing to notify admins before caps hit.
