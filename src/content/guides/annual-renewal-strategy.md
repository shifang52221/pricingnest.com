## Quick checklist
- Set renewal outreach 90 days before term end.
- Review usage and value delivered.
- Offer clear upgrade or downgrade paths.
- Keep renewal pricing consistent with policy.
- Track renewal churn separately.

## Step-by-step
1. Segment renewals by size and risk.
2. Review usage, support, and ROI.
3. Decide on renewal pricing and terms.
4. Communicate changes early.
5. Track renewal churn and expansion.

## What to watch
- Annual customers can hide early churn.
- Late outreach increases downgrade risk.
- Discounts at renewal reset price anchors.

## Common mistakes
- Treating renewals as new sales.
- Ignoring usage trends before renewal.
- Mixing renewal churn with logo churn.
