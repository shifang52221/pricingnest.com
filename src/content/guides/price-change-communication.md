## Quick checklist
- Announce changes at least one billing cycle ahead.
- Segment communication by plan and contract term.
- Offer a clear timeline and next steps.
- Provide a support path for objections.
- Track churn impact after rollout.

## Step-by-step
1. Define the price change date and scope.
2. Draft messaging for each segment.
3. Offer a grandfathering or lock option if needed.
4. Send reminders at 30 and 7 days.
5. Review churn impact monthly.

## What to watch
- Announcements that lack specific dates.
- Inconsistent messaging across channels.
- Support tickets that spike without staffing.

## Common mistakes
- Changing prices without a clear reason.
- Mixing marketing copy with legal terms.
- Announcing too close to renewal dates.
