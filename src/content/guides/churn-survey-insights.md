## Quick checklist
- Ask 3-5 short exit questions.
- Tag responses by plan and cohort.
- Separate price vs product reasons.
- Use findings to update tiers.
- Re-run surveys quarterly.

## Step-by-step
1. Add a short churn survey on cancellation.
2. Tag responses by plan and segment.
3. Quantify the top pricing objections.
4. Adjust limits or messaging.
5. Track churn impact after changes.

## What to watch
- Low response rates reduce signal quality.
- Free-form answers need structured tags.
- Pricing issues often show up as value gaps.

## Common mistakes
- Asking too many questions.
- Ignoring churn reasons for small plans.
- Treating all churn as price churn.
