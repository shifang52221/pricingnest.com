## Quick checklist
- Set a maximum discount by tier and segment.
- Translate discounts into effective monthly rates.
- Track churn response after discount programs.
- Require approval for enterprise exceptions.
- Review discount performance quarterly.

## Step-by-step
1. Define standard discount tiers for annual prepay.
2. Calculate effective monthly rates for each tier.
3. Model the revenue impact of discount changes.
4. Estimate churn lift from price sensitivity.
5. Set approval thresholds for exceptions.

## Guardrails that work
- Discounts tied to multi-year commitments.
- Usage-based plans with limited discounting.
- Clear expiry dates for promotional offers.

## Common mistakes
- Allowing sales to stack discounts unchecked.
- Forgetting to re-evaluate after price changes.
- Using discounts to mask weak packaging.
