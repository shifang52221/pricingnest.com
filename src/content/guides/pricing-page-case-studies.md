## Quick checklist
- Keep each case study under 120 words.
- Use real outcomes and a specific plan.
- Highlight the value metric in the story.
- Place cases below the plan cards.
- Avoid enterprise logos without permission.

## Step-by-step
1. Pick three customers that represent each tier.
2. Write a short problem-solution-outcome summary.
3. Include one metric tied to pricing value.
4. Add a callout that links to the relevant plan.
5. Review for accuracy with customer success.

## What to watch
- Long stories can bury the CTA.
- Generic outcomes reduce credibility.
- Mixing industries can dilute relevance.

## Common mistakes
- Using fake or anonymous results.
- Describing features instead of outcomes.
- Placing case studies above the plans.
