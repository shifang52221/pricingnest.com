## Quick checklist
- Segment campaigns by plan and usage.
- Align timing to renewal windows.
- Track campaign impact on churn.
- Use a consistent cadence each quarter.
- Keep messaging clear and short.

## Step-by-step
1. Build a calendar keyed to renewal dates.
2. Create campaign themes for each segment.
3. Add success metrics for each campaign.
4. Sync with product release timing.
5. Review results and adjust quarterly.

## What to watch
- Over-messaging leads to fatigue.
- Campaigns that do not match product value.
- Poor timing around renewal windows.

## Common mistakes
- Running the same campaign for all segments.
- Ignoring usage behavior in targeting.
- Measuring opens instead of retention.
