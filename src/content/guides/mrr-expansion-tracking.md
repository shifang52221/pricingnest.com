## Quick checklist
- Separate expansion from new MRR.
- Track expansion by plan and segment.
- Reconcile with billing data monthly.
- Compare expansion to churn impact.
- Monitor downgrade pressure.

## Step-by-step
1. Export starting MRR by cohort.
2. Sum expansion MRR from upgrades and add-ons.
3. Subtract churn and downgrades.
4. Calculate net new MRR.
5. Track trends by plan.

## What to watch
- Expansion masking rising churn.
- One-time fees inflating MRR.
- Mixing annual and monthly periods.

## Common mistakes
- Counting new logos as expansion.
- Ignoring contraction in expansion totals.
- Changing definitions between teams.
