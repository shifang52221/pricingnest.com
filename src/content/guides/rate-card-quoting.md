## Quick checklist
- Keep one rate card source of truth.
- Define unit names and billing cadence.
- Set discount limits by tier.
- Include minimum commitments if needed.
- Review rate cards quarterly.

## Step-by-step
1. Define core units and overage rates.
2. Map units to tiers and segments.
3. Set approved discount ranges.
4. Convert quotes to ARR and TCV.
5. Track deviations from the rate card.

## What to watch
- Custom quotes create pricing leakage.
- Missing units cause contract confusion.
- Over-discounting erodes margin.

## Common mistakes
- Using outdated rate cards in sales.
- Mixing one-off services into ARR.
- Skipping approvals on exceptions.
