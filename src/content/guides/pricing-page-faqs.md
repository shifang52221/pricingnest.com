## Quick checklist
- Answer the top 8 pricing objections.
- Include at least one usage example per tier.
- Clarify overages and caps in plain language.
- Keep answers short and concrete.
- Review FAQs after pricing changes.

## Step-by-step
1. List sales and support pricing questions.
2. Group questions by plan, usage, and billing.
3. Add one example bill per tier.
4. Explain overages and upgrade paths.
5. Keep the FAQ updated with each release.

## What to watch
- Ambiguous limits cause churn and chargebacks.
- Long FAQs reduce readability on mobile.
- Missing overage details drive distrust.

## Common mistakes
- Hiding key pricing rules in terms pages.
- Using internal jargon for value metrics.
- Mixing plan names and tier names.
