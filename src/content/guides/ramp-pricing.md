## Quick checklist
- Define the ramp duration and milestones.
- Tie ramp pricing to activation metrics.
- Set the post-ramp list price clearly.
- Model churn impact at ramp end.
- Document ramp eligibility rules.

## Step-by-step
1. Estimate time-to-value by segment.
2. Set a ramp price for the onboarding window.
3. Convert ramp price to full list price.
4. Model churn risk at renewal.
5. Monitor conversion after ramp ends.

## What to watch
- Long ramps reduce ARPA without retention gains.
- Unclear transitions trigger churn.
- Overlapping ramps complicate billing.

## Common mistakes
- Using ramp pricing as a permanent discount.
- Ignoring support cost during ramp.
- Skipping communication at ramp end.
