## Quick checklist
- Define partner discount bands.
- Protect gross margin on reseller deals.
- Align pricing with the public rate card.
- Require approval for exceptions.
- Review partner performance quarterly.

## Step-by-step
1. Set target margin thresholds for partners.
2. Create discount bands tied to deal size.
3. Align partner pricing with your rate card.
4. Add approval rules for deep discounts.
5. Track renewal and churn by partner channel.

## What to watch
- Partners discounting beyond agreed bands.
- Channel conflict with direct sales.
- Pricing promises that reduce renewal rates.

## Common mistakes
- Offering discounts without volume commitments.
- Hiding partner pricing from finance review.
- Using one discount band for all partners.
