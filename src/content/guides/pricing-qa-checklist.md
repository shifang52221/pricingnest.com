## Quick checklist
- Verify all plan prices and units.
- Confirm billing cycle labels and dates.
- Test the calculator defaults and outputs.
- Validate taxes and discounts in checkout.
- Check internal links and FAQs.

## Step-by-step
1. Validate all plan and add-on prices.
2. Review unit definitions and usage limits.
3. Test price changes in staging checkout.
4. Run a sample invoice for each plan.
5. Confirm pricing page links and CTAs.

## What to watch
- Old prices cached in marketing pages.
- Mismatched unit names across pages.
- Missing or outdated FAQ entries.

## Common mistakes
- Shipping pricing without cross-team review.
- Forgetting to update annual pricing copy.
- Leaving broken links after a migration.
